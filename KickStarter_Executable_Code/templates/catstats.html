Exploratory Data Analysis

Abstract

In addition to the Kickstarter Project Prediction Tool, we here provide you with meaningful insights via Exploratory Data Analysis on the datasets.

Dataset

We have collected information of over 600,000+ KickStarter projects comprising of 4 datasets obtained from Kaggle.
Note: Our analysis is done using the collected data, it does not reflect the actual representative of data on Kickstarter.

Statistics

Here we will be looking at Detailed Category-Wise Exploratory analysis on the datasets.
1.Mean Goal Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that Creators ask for unexpectedly high fundraiser amount in Technology, Film&Video, Art and Journalism resulting in failure.
2.Mean Amount Pledged Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that the Crowd tends to fund more on projects belonging to Technology, Design, Games, Food and Fashion categories.
3.Mean Number Of Backers Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that Crowd is more attracted towards projects belonging to Technology, Design, Games, Comic and Fashion categories
4.Mean Duration Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that projects having duration 30 days or under tend to be successful in each category.
5.Mean Number Of Reward Levels Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that Creator needs to provide atleast 8 reward levels to the crowd in almost every category for a Successful KickStarter Project.
6.Mean No. Of Facebook Friends Of Creator- Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us the number of FB-friends needed for project in each category that will help the Creator to get initial boost of funding and finally succeeding.
7.Mean No. Of Facebook Shares Of Creator's Project - Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us the number of FB-shares needed for project in each category , as the amount of shares are a good indicator that people are liking the project and an increase in the chance of funding.
8.Mean No. Of Creator's Projects Backed Previously - Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that categories like Game,Design ,Comics ,Fashion and Publishing require the creator to have a good history of projects backed compared to other categories for a succesful fundraiser.
9.Mean No. Of Images Uploaded Per Project- Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that categories like Games ,Design ,Technology ,Comics and Fashion reuires the creator to upload more images about the project compared to other categories for a succesful fundraiser.
10.Mean No. Of Words In Description Per Project- Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that categories like Games and Technology requires the creator to describe the project elaborately as projects of these categories are very complex and creator needs to make sure that there is no room for confusion among the crowd.
