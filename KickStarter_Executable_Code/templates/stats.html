Exploratory Data Analysis

Abstract

In addition to the Kickstarter Project Prediction Tool, we here provide you with meaningful insights via Exploratory Data Analysis on the datasets.

Dataset

We have collected information of over 600,000+ KickStarter projects comprising of 4 datasets obtained from Kaggle.
Note: Our analysis is done using the collected data, it does not reflect the actual representative of data on Kickstarter.

Statistics

Here we will be looking at brief overview of exploratory analysis on the datasets.
1.Percentage Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that the chance of successful crowdfunding on KickStarter is less but possible.
2.Percentage Of Successful vs Failed Projects Based On 15 categories of KickStarter
Analysis: This tells us that the Music , Comics , Theater and Dance categories have a higher chance of successful crowdfunding on KickStarter.
3.Mean Goal Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that having unexpedtedly high goal amount may result in a Failed KickStarter Project.
4.Mean Amount Pledged Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that if the crowd finds the project worthy, it can get pledges way beyond Mean Goal Amount.
5.Mean Duration Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that having high goal amount[3] and more number of days may result in a Failed KickStarter Project.
5.Mean No. Of Reward Levels Of Successful vs Failed Projects
Analysis: This tells us that Creator needs to provide atleast 8 reward levels to the crowd for a Successful KickStarter Project.
6.Mean No. Of Facebook Friends Of Creator- Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that Creator having more number of facebook friends has a good chance of being successsful as the creator gets a good initial boost of funding from his friends and family.
7.Mean No. Of Facebook Shares Of Creator's Project - Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that Project with more number of facebook shares has a good chance of being successsful as the amount of shares are a good indicator that people are liking the project.
8.Mean No. Of Creator's Projects Backed Previously - Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that Creator having a good history of projects created has a better chance of being successsful as it allows the crowd to verify the authenticity of the creator.
9.Mean No. Of Images Uploaded Per Project- Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that Creator uploading more images about the project has a better chance of being successsful as it allows the crowd to know more about what the project is going to be like.
10.Mean No. Of Words In Description Per Project- Successful vs Failed
Analysis: This tells us that Creator describing the project elaborately with more words has a better chance of being successsful as it reduces the confusion among the crowd regarding the project.
